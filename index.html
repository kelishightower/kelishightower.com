Spotlight

WACEFE Capstone

I’m currently working with a team to build Foundations of Wealth, a classroom-ready financial literacy dashboard that goes beyond budgeting to help students understand how wealth is shaped by systemic and historical factors. In partnership with WACEFE, we’re designing the platform to reflect Washington State’s unique economic landscape, connecting financial concepts to real-world data, policy, and patterns of access and opportunity. Through interactive dashboards, simulations, and scenario-based learning, we’re helping students see how larger systems influence personal financial outcomes—and how they can navigate those systems more effectively. Built with educator input, the platform is designed to integrate easily into classrooms while giving students both practical tools and meaningful context for their financial futures.
